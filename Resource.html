Introduction to Resource Outsourcing Solution

ISTL provides Resource Outsourcing services to our clients to help them quickly access specialized skills. ISTL offers this service on a project-by-project basis depending on the level of skill/expertise required for each project.

ISTL provides resource outsourcing across all verticals e.g. Banking, Insurance, Telecommunications, Manufacturing, Utilities etc.

Key Benefits of Resource Outsourcing solution

  • Enabling businesses to focus on core operations
  • Delivering cost savings – whether direct or indirect
  • Helping to create stable, cost-effective operations
  • Transferring focus from internal processes to achievement of business goals
  • Realizing investment in IT systems e.g. through API Management
  • Ensuring compliance with regulatory and best practice re­quirements
