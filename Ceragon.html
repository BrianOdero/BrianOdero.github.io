Ceragon – Microwave Solutions for Offshore Communications

Introduction to Ceragon – Microwave

ISTL, in collaboration with Ceragon, offers a state-of-the-art long-haul microwave system designed specifically for offshore communications.

Ceragon’s PointLink Long-Haul is a marine-grade microwave connectivity solution that delivers high capacity, low latency, and exceptional security, ensuring your offshore operations align with your business goals.

This advanced system combines reliable microwave technology with bespoke antenna stabilization and resilient network paths, offering unmatched reliability and peace of mind.

The PointLink Long-Haul is engineered to perform under extreme conditions, featuring an innovative stabilization system and customized software integration. Its antenna utilizes cutting-edge GPS motion sensors to maintain precise pointing accuracy, even in challenging sea conditions.

Benefits Of Ceragon Microwave solution

  • Durability in Extreme Marine Environments: Engineered to withstand the harshest conditions at sea.
  • ATEX Zone 1 Certification: Safe for use in highly explosive environments.
  • Guaranteed Bandwidth: Built-in Quality of Service (QoS) mechanisms ensure reliable performance for mission-critical services.
  • Designed for High-Capacity Backbone Networks: Optimized for long-distance communication in low frequency bands.

The PointLink Long-Haul is an ideal choice for various offshore applications, including remote operations, crew infotainment for mobile platforms, drilling rigs, floating production units (FPSOs), and storage and offload ships. Leveraging Ceragon’s FibeAir IP-20 platform, it provides high availability, low latency, and superior capacity for your offshore connectivity needs.
