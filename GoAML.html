GoAML Solution

Introduction to GoAML

GoAML is a specialized reporting tool developed by ISTL for generating compliance reports required by FRC and UNODC. This guide explores the key features and benefits of GoAML.

What does GoAML do?

GoAML automates the creation of compliance reports, offering a comprehensive solution for monitoring company performance trends and ensuring regulatory compliance.

Key Components of GoAML

  • Reporting Tool: Generates CTR, STR, and SAR reports in XML format.
  • Data Integration: Fetches data from core sources via DB-link or file-based methods.
  • Compliance Automation: Ensures compliance with FRC and UNODC regulations.
  • Enhanced Functionality: Provides both on-demand and automated reporting.
