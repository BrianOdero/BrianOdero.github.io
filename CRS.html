About Us

Introduction to Common Reporting Standards (CRS)

In today's regulatory environment, financial institutions are required to identify and report information on accounts held by foreign tax residents to their respective tax authorities. ISTL's CRS solution is designed to streamline this process by automatically generating the required reports in XML format for submission to Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA).

What is the CRS Solution?

ISTL's CRS is a comprehensive reporting tool that integrates seamlessly with core banking applications and various product processors, such as treasury, trade, lending, internet banking, cards, fixed assets, HR, and CRM. It fetches data through DB-link or file-based methods, ensuring accurate and efficient data collection. The process includes; Indicia identification, Automated data extraction, Review of the extracted data, XML report generation for upload to the KRA portal.

Key Components of CRS

  • Seamless Integration: Connect with existing systems without disruptions.
  • Automated Data Collection: Aggregates data automatically, eliminating manual entry.
  • Real-Time Compliance Monitoring: Adapts to evolving standards with built-in compliance checks.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simplifies the reporting process for all users.
  • Maker Checker Editing Option: Ensures data accuracy and validation.
  • Case Management: Manages exception reports efficiently.
