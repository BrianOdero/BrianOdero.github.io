WHAT IS SASAHEALTH?

ISTL's Sasa Health is a multi-module hospital management system that leverages information technology to manage various aspects of hospital operations, patient care, and administrative functions, thus enabling end-to-end process automation

Key Features of ISTL's Sasa Health:

  • Standalone modules for emergency, claim management, and asset disposal allowing hospitals to tailor the system to their specific needs and streamline their operations.
  • At the end of each year, the system enables closure of accounts, which helps to ensure accurate financial reporting and prevents errors.
  • DICOM server interface: The radiology module can be interfaced with a DICOM server, which allows for the seamless integration of medical images into the system.
  • Insurance company API integration: The system can be easily integrated with insurance company APIs, which streamlines the claims process and reduces administrative burden.
  • Market referral and incentive management: The system has the capability to manage market referrals and incentives, which can help hospitals to attract new patients and increase revenue.

Benefit of Using ISTL's Sasa Health:

  • Efficient patient information management of patient records, which improves data accuracy and accessibility
  • Improved patient care By providing quick access to patient history and all other patient information, healthcare providers can make quick and informed decisions, leading to improved treatment.
  • Enhanced decision-making hospital administrators can use the reports generated by the system to make informed strategic decisions, which will also lead to streamlined administrative processes.
  • Billing and financial management - Automated billing processes reduce the chances of billing errors, increasing the accuracy of financial transactions and aiding in revenue tracking.
