e-KYC(Know Your Customer)

Introduction to e-KYC

In today's digital age, ISTL's advanced e-KYC solution revolutionizes the customer onboarding process. This guide delves into the features, benefits, and implementation of our e-KYC solution.

What is e-KYC?

ISTL's e-KYC solution streamlines the digital onboarding process, ensuring a secure and seamless experience across all platforms. It leverages cutting-edge technology to facilitate real-time sanction and PEP screenings via the Icomply App

Key Components of e-KYC

  • APIs: Integrates government information verification, mobile number validation, and KRA pin checker.
  • SDKs: Utilizes OCR for photo recognition and biometric capture for enhanced security.
  • Icomply App: Enables individuals to self-onboard securely on any device.

Benefits of e-KYC solution

Cost-Effective

Lowers costs associated with manual verification processes.

Enhanced Security

Protects sensitive information with robust security measures.

Improved Efficiency

Automates monitoring and reporting processes.

Scalability

Handles large volumes of verifications efficiently.

Convenience

Remote onboarding eliminates the need for physical presence.

Speed

Accelerates the onboarding process, reducing turnaround time.

Accuracy

Ensures precise identification using advanced OCR and biometric technologies.

Compliance

Adheres to regulatory standards by verifying identities against official databases.

Use Case: Icomply

Icomply, a financial institution, adopted ISTL's e-KYC solution for secure digital onboarding. Using APIs and SDKs for verification and biometric capture, Icomply streamlined the process with an intuitive interface, enabling customers to self-onboard via smartphones.
