Granular Data Integration (GDI) Platform

Introduction to GDI Platform

In response to new regulatory requirements from the Central Bank of Kenya, financial institutions need to automate their data retrieval processes to ensure compliance and avoid penalties. ISTL’s GDI platform offers a seamless solution to help organizations efficiently retrieve, review, and submit their data.

What is the GDI Platform?

ISTL’s GDI platform is a robust automation solution designed to help financial institutions comply with regulatory requirements. It simplifies data integration, review, and submission processes, ensuring accurate and timely reporting.

Key Components of the GDI platform

  • Seamless Data Integration: Connect to existing systems and databases for comprehensive data management.
  • Review and Confirmation: Review and confirm data accuracy before submission.
  • Submission-Ready Reports: Automatically compile relevant data into review-ready reports.
  • User-Friendly Dashboard: Access key metrics and insights for informed decision-making.
